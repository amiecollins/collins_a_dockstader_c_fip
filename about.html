About Amie Collins

Amie Collins is a twenty-one-year-old Fanshawe College student from London, Ontario. She has been playing video games since pre-school and even aspires to one day help make games, either in the indie or the triple-A studios. Her favorite game is The Last of Us by Naughty Dog, but was originally inspired to join the game development field by Batman: Arkham Asylum. She even studied game development for two years at the University of Ontario Institute of Technology.

She has three cats and two dogs at home and is a big animal lover. Her goals include becoming a front-end designer or a product designer. Basically anything with designer in the title. Yes she is still looking for her true niche, as technology and creativity are something that blossoms in her heart.

About Cheyenne Dockstader

Cheyenne Dockstader is a nineteen year old college student attending Fanshawe college for the Interactive media design in 3D technology program. Born and raised in London, Ontario, she is working to become a part of the animation industry. As a child watching her older siblings playing the Nintendo was the best part of a five year old’s life. Once she became older, instead of watching others play video games she tried her hand at it herself. She discovered a true love for Nintendo games. Playing and experiencing the intricacies of such well put together games sparked the desire to create her own stories and universes and build worlds for other gamers to enjoy.
